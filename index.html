Banking Analytics · Retention Strategy

Customer Engagement & Product Utilization Analytics for Retention Strategy

A banking analytics project examining customer behavior, engagement levels, and product usage to identify factors influencing customer retention. Using the European Bank Customer dataset, the project analyzes demographics, financial commitment, and banking activity to detect churn patterns and surface high-value customers at risk of leaving.
